Best and Worst Openings

This page is meant to be reminiscent of the Isotropic leaderboard. We consider the openings -- the cards you buy on the first two turns -- as if they were players whose skill adds to yours. The "level" of an opening then indicates the minimum amount it should add, or subtract, from your effective level.

We define Silver/Silver to have a skill of 0. The many openings that have not distinguished themselves from Silver/Silver (which appear in gray between "level +0" and "level -0") are sorted by average skill instead of level.
